About me

My name is Czicken and I'm a -year-old amateur artist based in the Czech republic, who also dabbles in a bit of coding. The majority of my work is digital but there might be a couple attempts at traditional art mixed in too. I use Photoshop, which has been my preferred program for 6 years now.

My portfolio features all my artwork dating back as far as 2020. My old art is a bit... lacking but I personally believe that as embarrassing as old drawings can be, it's nice to see the journey of how one developed their current style.
